YULIIA PYLYPCHUK

ABOUT ME

My name is Yulia. I am a young artist from the
south of Ukraine, currently living in Tallinn. Art
is my way of exploring the world and
expressing myself. I work in painting and love
experimenting with color, textures and shapes.

I have participated in several projects in recent
years: I created an art object for the art store
Skizze from used brushes, was part of an artist
fair at ARS Kunstilinnak and took part in a
photo project. I will have an exhibition soon
and I am looking forward to it.

I am currently studying at Tartu Kunsti Kool on a
one-year course, but my path in art began long
before that – I have been attending an art studio
for four years now, where I am developing my
style and technique.

I'm here to share my art and inspire.
